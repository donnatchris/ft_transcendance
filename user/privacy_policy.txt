Privacy Policy

Last updated: [08/18/2025]

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Data we collect

Email address: required to create your account.
Login, display name, status and password hash: required for authentication and gameplay.
Optional profile data: avatar.
Game-related data: match history and personal statistics.
We do not collect or process any other personal data.

2. How we use your data

To manage your account and allow you to play.
To contact you only in case of issues related to your account.
To ensure security and prevent abuse.
Your email address is never shared with other users and is never transferred to third parties.

3. Data storage and security

Passwords are stored only as secure cryptographic hashes (never in plain text).
The email address is stored in our database but never made public.
Technical measures are applied to protect your data against unauthorized access.

4. Data retention

Your account and data will be automatically deleted after 6 months of inactivity.
You can request deletion of your account at any time.
When your account is deleted, all personal information and related data (friends, matches, statistics) are also deleted or anonymized.

5. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
Access: you can request to see what data we hold about you.
Rectification: you can modify your personal information at any time.
Erasure: you can delete your account and personal data permanently.
Restriction and objection: you can limit or object to certain uses of your data.
To exercise your rights, please contact us at: [Your contact email]

6. Cookies

We use cookies only for authentication purposes (session token).
Cookies are marked as HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite=strict.
No tracking or third-party cookies are used.

7. Legal basis

We process your email and login information on the basis of:
Contractual necessity: to provide you with an account and game access.
Legitimate interest: to ensure account security and prevent fraud.
